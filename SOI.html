The Start of Insanity

Even the smallest numbers in googology are still hard to comprehend, let's start with 52 Factorial. 52 Factorial (Commonly written as 52!) is the number of possible orientations in a singular deck of cards, this doesn’t seem very hard to comprehend, but let’s set an example. Set a timer for 52! (8.07 E+67) seconds, then pick your favorite spot on the equator. And wait 1 Billion years and take one step forward, then wait another Billion years and take another step, repeat this until you’ve lapped the equator. Then take a single drop of water out of the ocean, repeat this until the ocean is drained. We're done now right?, nope, refill the ocean and place a piece of paper down. Repeat all prior steps and place another piece of paper, repeat this step till your stack of papers reaches the sun. You look at your timer and realize you still have 8.07 E+67 seconds. You would have to repeat this process one thousand times till you're done. Except you’re not done, you're only a third of the way to being finished. To pass the rest of the time, let's play another game. Take a deck of cards and start shuffling them, every billion years deal yourself a 5 card poker hand, every time you get a royal flush go buy a lottery ticket, every time you win, put a piece of sand in the Grand Canyon, once the canyon is filled take

an ounce of rock from Mount Everest, once Mount Everest is completely leveled you STILL won’t be done. Repeat all the steps starting from the deck of cards 264 times, only then will you finally be done with the timer.
