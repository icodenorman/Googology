What is Googology?

Googology is the study and exploration of exceptionally large numbers and fast-growing functions, most numbers here are impossible to comprehend or impossible to even write out, and most of these numbers are impossible to prove that it is finite. Let's talk about infinity, Infinity is not the biggest number, the entire point of Googology is to find the biggest finite value, infinity does not count.
